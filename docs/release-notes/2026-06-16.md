## Subject

What's new — a guided start, and a lot of your feedback

## Body

Thanks for testing and filing such detailed feedback — most of this release came straight from it.

The big one: a guided start. When you log in, a short step-by-step panel now walks you through your first real task for your role, in plain language. Skip it anytime — it only appears once.

- Chapter officers: add a researcher, set their available time, put them on a project.
- Working-group leaders: take a project into your group, then post the roles it needs.
- Members: find your tasks and keep your availability current.
- Each step explains the why (what STR is, what a "card" is) as you go.

Finding your work and your way around:

- "My tasks" is now in your account menu (top-right), so your worklist is always one click away.
- Clicking your STR balance now actually opens your wallet (it used to change the address bar but leave you on the same page).
- STR has a "?" beside it that explains what it is; a member "card" is now spelled out on the People page.
- The Projects page header now counts active projects and links shipped ones to the Hall of Fame, so an empty list no longer reads as "you searched wrong".
- The top-right "Admin" area gathers what's waiting on your review.

For working-group leaders:

- A new "Projects looking for a working group" section lets you adopt an unassigned project into your group — that's what unlocks editing it and posting its needs. You can also start a new project under your group.

Safer, clearer actions:

- Changing a project's status, assigning someone to a role, or finishing a project now asks you to confirm first, and seats can be removed/undone.
- One single skill scale everywhere (the old badges are retired), and the Guide is reorganized around what each role actually does.
- Mobile layout and dark mode are cleaned up.

Keep it coming — every issue you open turns into a fix and a test so it stays fixed.
